The Problem

Your team uses six AI providers. You manage six bills, six SLAs, six compliance reviews.

The market told you that adopting AI was easy. Then your engineering org integrated OpenAI for reasoning, Anthropic for long context, Google for multimodal, and three more for cost arbitrage. Now your procurement team is drowning.

Vendor lock-in

Switching from one provider to another means rewriting integrations and re-auditing every workflow. The migration cost gets buried in engineering cycles your CFO never sees.

Cost opacity

Token pricing changes monthly. Your finance team has no way to forecast next quarter's AI spend, and your engineering team has no incentive to choose the cheapest capable model.

Reliability gaps

When a provider degrades, your application degrades with it. There is no failover. There is no second source. Your customers feel it before your monitoring does.
