FAQ

Common pricing questions

What if I exceed my token allowance?
Overage is billed at the underlying provider rate plus a 12% routing margin. You can set a hard cap that stops traffic before overage triggers, or a soft cap that sends an alert and continues. No surprise invoices.
Can I bring my own provider account?
Yes. On the Business and Enterprise plans, you can attach your existing OpenAI, Anthropic, or other provider account and Mist will route through it. You keep your existing relationship; you get the routing fabric on top. Some compliance features require Mist’s own provider relationships — we’ll explain the trade-offs during onboarding.
Do you support data residency?
Yes, on the Enterprise plan. We support US-only, EU-only, and APAC-only routing. Data residency constraints become part of the routing decision — Mist will exclude any provider that cannot serve the request from the required region.
How does billing work for usage above the base plan?
You’re billed monthly in arrears for any tokens beyond your annual allowance. Overage is itemized by provider and model on your invoice, with full reconciliation to your audit log. Annual contracts can be true-up at renewal if your run-rate has shifted.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a 14-day proof-of-concept for qualified Business and Enterprise prospects, including up to 10M tokens of routed traffic and a sales engineer to help with the integration. We don’t offer self-serve free tiers — we’re built for procurement-led adoption.
Do you charge for failed requests?
No. If a routing attempt fails and Mist falls back to another provider, you’re only billed for the successful response. If all fallbacks fail, you’re not charged.
Can I switch tiers mid-contract?
Yes, upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at renewal. No cancellation fees.
