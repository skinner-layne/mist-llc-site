About this page

This status page reflects direct health checks against each upstream provider's inference endpoint. A provider marked operational is responding to minimal requests within expected latency bounds. A provider marked degraded is responding with elevated latency or intermittent errors. A provider marked down is failing health checks entirely.

Mist Dynamics abstracts provider instability via automatic failover across fallback chains. In most cases, customer traffic continues uninterrupted even when an individual provider is down. For incident updates and scheduled maintenance, subscribe to status notifications at contact.mist.llc.
